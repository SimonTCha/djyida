About

The Man
Behind
The Decks

DJ Yida is a hip-hop DJ and TikTok sensation who's been turning up venues and timelines alike. With roots in the underground scene and a natural feel for the crowd, Yida blends trap, hip-hop, and Afrobeats into sets that never let you catch your breath.

From sold-out club nights to massive festivals, Yida has established himself as one of the most exciting DJs on the circuit — and his viral TikTok presence keeps the culture moving online just as hard as in the room.
